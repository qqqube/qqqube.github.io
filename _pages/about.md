Hello, my name is Spencer! I am currently a PhD student at the National University of Singapore (NUS).


Previously, I spent time as a visiting researcher at Stony Brook University (advised by [Ting Wang](https://alps-lab.github.io/)) and Tsinghua University (supervised by [Zhiyuan Liu](https://nlp.csai.tsinghua.edu.cn/~lzy/)). I also had the opportunity to work with [Junfeng Yang](https://www.cs.columbia.edu/~junfeng/) and [Asaf Cidon](https://www.asafcidon.com/) while earning my Master's.

I received my Bachelor's degrees from UC Berkeley and have an M.S. in Computer Science from Columbia University.
